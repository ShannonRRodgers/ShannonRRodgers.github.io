Short Bio

My name is Shannon Rodgers, I am a current first year PhD student in Human Computer Interaction at the Queensland University of Technology. I am currently living and researching in Illinois, USA. My current research interests are older adult gardeners, phenology, HCI and IoT technologies.

Previously I obtained my Bachelor in IT (Computer Science) with honours in the school of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and the Queensland University of Technology.
